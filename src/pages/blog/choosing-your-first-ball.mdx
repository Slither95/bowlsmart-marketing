# Choosing Your First Arsenal Ball

Starting your high school bowling journey? Here's a quick, no-jargon guide to picking the right first ball.

## 1. Know your style
- **Speed**: Faster throwers often prefer stronger cores.
- **Rev rate**: Higher revs shine with pearl or hybrid covers.

## 2. Start versatile
A good first ball is a hybrid coverstock with a symmetric core. It handles a wide range of lane conditions.
